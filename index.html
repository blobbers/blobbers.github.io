MUST TRY FOODS IN VIETNAM

We based this rating off of a survey done by many Vietnamese people as well as others ethnic groups.

1. Pho

Pho is the most well known Vietnamese dish. With thousands of restaurants all over the world, the flavor that this dish contains has stolen the hearts of people from all over the world.

2. Bun Bo

Many of the well known Vietnamese dishes are noodle dishes packed with tons of flavors. Bun Bo is another noodle dish that is very well known in many parts of the world.
