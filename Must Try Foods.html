TOP 10 MUST TRY FOODS IN VIETNAM

1. Pho

This special dish has stolen hearts of people from all around the world. With over thousands of different Pho restaurants surrounding the globe. This dish has exploded and became the most popular Vietnamese dish we know today.

2. Bun Cha Ha Noi

This dish is one of the most common and one of the most well-known Vietnamese dishes outside of Vietnam. With Vietnam divided into 3 regions, North, South, and Central there are different variations of the same dish. This is the North's variation of grill pork noodle.

3. Banh Xeo

Many people think of this dish as a Vietnamese pancake or a sizzling cake. With the inside filled of pork, shrimp, and beansprouts this dish is usually eaten with fish sauce and is very popular around the world.

4. Bun Rieu

Topping the charts as one of the most popular foods in Vietnam is Bun Rieu. This dish has a lot of variations. Based on the region people will cook it with crab, fish, snails, and sometimes even tofu. With the different variations this is a very unique and lovable dish.

5. Com Bi Suon Cha Trung

Rice is an ingredient that is used all over the world and is loved by millions from different ethnic backgrounds. In Vietnam rice is eaten on a daily bases. But the most popular rice dish in Vietnam is rice with grilled pork chop and a special sweet and sour sauce. Not only is this dish loved in Vietnam but it is also loved by people from all over the world.
